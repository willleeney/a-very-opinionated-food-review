Design Summary

Filter patterns

  • Type: C4 chips (orange/terracotta active) + dropdown
  • Value: Range slider (min rating)
  • Taste: Range slider (min rating)
  • From: Underline tabs with orgs
  • Sliders show "Any" or "6+" etc as value label
  • Dropdown has rounded corners (8px)
  • Subtle shadow, terracotta accents throughout

Social filter options

  • Everyone — all reviews
  • Following — people you follow
  • [Org names] — your organisations
  • Just me — only your reviews
  • Orgs appear dynamically based on membership
  • Single-select (mutually exclusive)

Design Recommendations

Based on your editorial design philosophy, I'd recommend:

  • Category filters: Editorial Chips (Option C) — matches existing button style, supports multi-select
  • Rating filters: Segment Button Bars (Option B) — one-tap, clear state, good scannability
  • Following filter: Button Group (Option A) — explicit, familiar, no ambiguity
  • Add place: Icon+label category buttons, dual rating number buttons with color coding

The "prose style" alternative is more editorial but less scannable. Could work for a landing page hero but probably too clever for daily use.
